School Devision

For students of class 9 to 12

Key features

  1. Target Audience: High school (Class-IX to XII) students.
  2. Challenges: Participants will focus on age-appropriate
    problems and imaginative solutions.
  3. Skill Level: Competitors in this group will have the
    opportunity to develop and showcase their problem-solving skills.
  4. Prerequisites: School-level students with a
    passion for innovation and a desire to learn are encouraged to participate.

Team details: Each team participating can consist of 3 to 5 members.

Prize Details: Prize Money and Memento for the winners.
